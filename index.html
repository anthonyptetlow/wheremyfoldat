Where my fold at?

What is the fold?

The fold is the horizontal line dictated by the bottom of the screen before a user scrolls. It was traditionally though that this screan realstage is high value and the most important information should be placed here.

What does “Where my fold at” do?

“Where my fold at” pulls data from the Google Analytics API in order to find out the screen resolutions of devices and display them for you to see where your fold is, if it exists at all.

Step 1 - Authentication

Firstly you will need to allow wheremyfloldat to access to your google analytics data. Don’t worry we never store your analytics data as all the communication is direct between your browser and the google analytics api.
